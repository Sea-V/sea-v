About SEA-V

A modern digital hub for seafarers

The Idea

SEA-V was created to give seafarers a modern way to manage and present their professional careers.

For too long, maritime professionals have relied on scattered paperwork, outdated CVs, and fragmented records of sea time, certificates, and vessel history.

SEA-V brings everything together in one place — a clean, structured digital profile built specifically for life at sea.

What SEA-V Does

SEA-V allows seafarers to track and manage their maritime career in one secure platform.

  • Log and calculate sea time
  • Store certificates and expiry dates
  • Track vessel history
  • Collect professional references
  • Create a clean digital maritime profile

A Better Professional Profile

Instead of sending outdated PDF CVs or scattered documents, SEA-V aims to provide a professional maritime profile that clearly shows experience, qualifications, and career progression.

The vision is simple: a platform where maritime professionals can confidently present their career in a way that is structured, transparent, and easy to verify.

Whether you're deck, engineering, interior, or bridge team, SEA-V is designed to evolve with your career.

The Vision

SEA-V is just getting started.

The long-term goal is to build a trusted digital ecosystem for seafarers — where careers can be tracked, verified, and shared with confidence across the maritime industry.

Future developments aim to include verification tools, professional networking, and better ways for crew and industry professionals to connect.

At its core, SEA-V is about supporting the people who build their lives at sea.
