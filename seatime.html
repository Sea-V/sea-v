PYA / MCA testimonial checklist Open when preparing sea service for verification or CoC applications.
  • Record both start and end dates for every engagement — even if still onboard.
  • Do not overlap dates between entries; new testimonials should follow the last period.
  • Break down actual sea, standby, and yard service using MCA definitions.
  • Watchkeeping days should reflect approved watchkeeping service only.
  • Attach signed sea service testimonials (SST) — Captain, or Owner/Manager for Masters.
  • Use the PYA or Nautilus portal links above for official verification, or export CSV for your training manager.
